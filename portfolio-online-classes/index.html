Interactive Recipes

Spicy Grilled Fish

Follow this simple recipe to recreate the spicy grilled fish at home.

  1. Clean and marinate the fish with chili, garlic, and lime juice.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  3. Grill the fish for 5-7 minutes on each side until cooked through.
  4. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve hot.

Traditional Rice Cake

Learn how to make the traditional rice cake with this easy step-by-step guide.

  1. Soak glutinous rice overnight.
  2. Steam the rice until soft and sticky.
  3. Shape the rice into cakes and let cool.
  4. Serve with coconut milk or palm sugar syrup.
